# Stress-Sleep Mediation Analysis

## Overview
This project investigates the impact of stress on sleep quality, inspired by the study:

> Zhang J, Xiang S, Li X, Tang Y, Hu Q (2024). *The impact of stress on sleep quality: a mediation analysis based on longitudinal data*. Frontiers in Psychology, 15:1431234. [DOI: 10.3389/fpsyg.2024.1431234](https://doi.org/10.3389/fpsyg.2024.1431234)

We reproduced key analyses from the paper and adapted them to our dataset.

## Objectives
- Reproduce findings from Zhang et al. (2024)  
- Analyze mediating effects of rumination, social anxiety, emotion-focused coping strategies, and mobile phone dependency  
- Explore additional insights using our longitudinal dataset  

## Data
- Longitudinal survey of 426 university students  
- Variables: Stress, Rumination, Social Anxiety, Emotion-focused Coping, Mobile Phone Dependency, Sleep Quality  

## Methodology
1. Data Cleaning & Preprocessing  
2. Exploratory Data Analysis  
3. Mediation Analysis (using structural equation modeling or regression)  
4. Visualization of relationships and mediation effects
